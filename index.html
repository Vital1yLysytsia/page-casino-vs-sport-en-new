Official rules

Football bets are the recommended option for beginners in the world of betting.

At first, football bets were a form of entertainment. For example, we can mention the totalizer — an ancient system of pools that originated in Britain at the beginning of the 20th century with the following conditions:

  • At the stadiums, fans bought tickets at the minimum price (later tickets began to be distributed through newspapers, where they were cut out);
  • On the cards, fans wrote the results of future matches “game number one”, which they considered the most likely;
  • Coupons were transferred to the pool collector or sent by mail to the organizing company.

A specialized company guaranteed that there would be at least one winner every week. The winners were determined by the number of points scored by all fans who sent their cards.

The totalizer system allowed you to earn big money by making your first football bets. For example, Michael Elliott, risking only 2 pounds sterling, earned over 3 million pounds. He managed to correctly predict 8 matches that ended in a draw 2:2.

The pool system was successful until “bookmakers” began to appear in the form they exist today. Fans “game number one” got the opportunity to make bets with much more comfort.
